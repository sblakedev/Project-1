Our Classes

We offer two classes for parents and caregivers. Our classes are an ideal place to meet other parents and caregivers, get support and parenting advice, and give your child your undivided attention, away from the distractions at home.

Baby Class

Our Baby Classes are aimed at babies up to 18 months old. Parents and caregivers are encouraged to help baby explore and learn about their world through song, play and storytime. We incorporate baby massage at the end of each class so that baby is nice and relaxed going home.

Toddler Class

Our Toddler Classes are aimed at children between 18 months and 3 years old. Together we sing, dance, do arts and crafts and act out stories. We then wind down with 20 minutes of sensory play at the end of the class.
